Section 1: Scope of Work
AcmeCorp will provide software development services including frontend and backend development.

Section 2: Payment Terms
Payments shall be made on a monthly basis upon invoice submission.

Section 3: Non-Disclosure Agreement
All developers working on the project must sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) before commencing work.

Section 4: Working Hours
The development team shall be available from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday.

Section 5: Payment rate
Fixed rate = $100/hour * (experience level + 1)
Rate = Fixed rate + 20% for overtime
Bonus = Rate * 0.1
Total = Rate + Bonus
